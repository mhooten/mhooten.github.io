Leach, C.B., P.J. Williams, J.M. Eisaguirre, J.N. Womble, M.R. Bower, and M.B. Hooten. (2022). Recursive Bayesian computation facilitates adaptive optimal design in ecological studies. Ecology, 103: e03573.
